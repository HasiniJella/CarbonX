🌱 NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index)

Based on:

Sentinel-2 satellite imagery

Using bands:

B8 → Near Infrared (NIR)
B4 → Red

Formula:

NDVI=
NIR+RED
NIR−RED
	​


What it tells:

vegetation health
greenness
crop vitality
forest density

Typical values:

NDVI	Meaning
0–0.2	barren
0.2–0.4	sparse vegetation
0.4–0.6	healthy crops
0.6–0.9	dense vegetation
